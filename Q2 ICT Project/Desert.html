Ingredients:

Vegetable Oil, White Onions, chopped, Garlic, chopped Chicken Thigh Fillet,120 g Chinese Sausage, sliced diagonal thin 50 g Squid Balls, cut into half 120 g Pork, Shoulder or Butt, (Kasim), cut into strips of 1/4" cubes 80 g Carrots, julienne 100 g Shitake Mushroom, sliced 100 g Snow Peas, halved lengthwise 100 g Knorr Oyster Flavoured Sauce 3.6kg 150 g

Instructions:

  • Heat the vegetable oil in a wok and add the onions and garlic. Stir-fry for a minute before adding the pork kasim;cook until it turns golden.
  • Add chicken and cook for 1 minute followed by adding in the Chinese sausage and squid balls.
  • Stir-fry for 2 minutes then proceed to add the vegetables and Knorr Oyster Sauce; fry for 1 minute.
  • Next, pour the water, dark soy sauce, pepper and Knorr Pork Broth Base.
  • Bring to a boil and simmer for 3 minutes. Remove the pre-cooked vegetables and set them aside.
  • Add the dried egg noodles. After cooking it for 8 minutes, throw in the cabbage and snow peas.
  • Stir-fry over high heat until all the moisture has evaporated. Place the pre-cooked vegetables back in the wok and cook for a minute.
  • Serve and garnish with egg, tofu, spring onions, and crispy garlic.
